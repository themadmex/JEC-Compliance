Human Resource Security Policy

1. Objective

To ensure that employees and contractors understand their responsibilities and are suitable for the roles they are considered for.

2. Background Checks

Verification checks on all candidates for employment shall be carried out in accordance with relevant laws, regulations, and ethics.

2.4 Level 3 Security Clearance

For positions requiring Level 3 clearance, background checks must include a 5-year criminal history check and professional reference verification from at least two previous employers.

3. Terms of Employment

The contractual agreement with employees and contractors shall state their and the organization's responsibilities for information security.

Human Resource Security Policy

1. Objective

To ensure that employees and contractors understand their responsibilities and are suitable for the roles they are considered for.

2. Background Checks

Verification checks on all candidates for employment shall be carried out in accordance with relevant laws, regulations, and ethics.

2.4 Level 3 Security Clearance

Updated

For positions requiring Level 3 clearance, background checks must now include a comprehensive 10-year criminal history check, global financial sanctions screening, and professional reference verification from at least four previous employers or equivalent professional contacts.

info Increased from 5 years and 2 references to align with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 standards.

3. Terms of Employment

The contractual agreement with employees and contractors shall state their and the organization's responsibilities for information security.
